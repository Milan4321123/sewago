Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

SewaGo ("we", "us") operates a rides, food delivery, hotel booking and mini-jobs platform in Nepal. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. It applies to the SewaGo customer, driver and partner apps and to sewago websites.

What we collect

  • Account details — name, email address, phone number and a hashed password. We never store your password in readable form.
  • Location — pickup and drop-off points you choose; for drivers, live GPS position while online so nearby ride requests can be matched and customers can see their driver approaching. Location sharing stops when a driver goes offline.
  • Transactions — wallet top-ups, payments, earnings, withdrawals and the ledger entries needed to run your balance. Card or wallet credentials are handled by the payment gateways (eSewa, Khalti); we never see or store them.
  • Verification data — driver licence reference and vehicle details, and partner business registration details, used only to keep the marketplace safe.
  • Activity — your rides, orders, stays and tasks, so both sides of each booking see the same history and disputes can be resolved.

How we use it

  • To provide the service: matching rides, delivering orders, managing bookings and moving money through your SewaGo wallet.
  • To keep the platform safe: verifying drivers and partners, preventing fraud and keeping an audit trail of sensitive staff actions.
  • To contact you about your account: one-time codes by SMS, password reset links by email, and booking updates in the app. We do not send marketing without consent.

Who we share it with

  • The other side of your booking — a driver sees your name, pickup and drop-off; you see the driver's name, vehicle and plate. Restaurants and hotels see what they need to fulfil your order or stay. Nothing more.
  • Service providers — payment gateways (eSewa, Khalti) to process payments, an SMS provider to deliver verification codes, an email provider to deliver password resets, and our hosting and database providers. Each receives only what it needs.
  • Authorities — only when the law requires it.
  • We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

Retention and deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from inside the app (Profile → Delete account). Deletion permanently removes your name, email, phone number and verification details. Anonymised transaction records are retained where financial record-keeping requires it.

Your choices

  • Location permission can be revoked in your phone settings (rides need it to work).
  • You can correct your phone number and account details in the app.
  • You can request a copy of your data, or its deletion, at the contact below.

Security

All traffic is encrypted over HTTPS. Passwords are stored as salted scrypt hashes, session tokens expire automatically, and staff actions on payments and verifications are logged in an append-only audit trail.
